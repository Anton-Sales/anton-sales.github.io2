What is grid based design?

A grid based design serves as a framework. Websites are broken into grids, and in these grids, graphic elements (images, paragraphs, videos, etc.) keeps the content organized in a rational, easy to absorb manner.

Whats all the hype about responsive webpages?

Nowadays, people use many different devices to access the internet, whether it being a desktop, laptop, netbooks, tablets, and phones. more and more people every year are accessing the internet primarily through the their tablet or mobile phones. All these devices have a different size of screen (resolution). Responsive webpages are the sites able to adapt to the screen, despite what device is being used.

What is semantic structure?

Semantic structre is the means of describing the content in a webpage. As an example, h1, img, and form, tags, makes sense to a browser, and all the users looking at the code.
